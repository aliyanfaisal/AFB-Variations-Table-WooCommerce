An Awesome WordPress WooCommerce Plugin to change usual Variations Dropdown on the product page to a nice and modern looking Table.

And Built-in Prcing Tiers System, that enables you to dynamically set the price of a product based on its quantity being selected.
You can create different tiers and set the discount rate on each tier.

E.g for 20 quantity of a product 10% discount or 100 discount
